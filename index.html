Company Overview

Built on a Foundation of Quality & Trust

DVR Fab Tech was established with a singular mission: to provide India's industrial sector with world-class colour coated steel roofing solutions at competitive prices. Since our inception, we have grown to become one of the most reliable and respected names in the steel roofing industry.

Our manufacturing facility is equipped with cutting-edge roll forming machinery, enabling us to produce a wide range of roofing profiles with exceptional dimensional accuracy and surface quality. We serve clients across sectors including agriculture, warehousing, retail, and heavy industry.

Every product that leaves our facility undergoes rigorous quality testing — from coating adhesion and salt spray resistance to paint thickness and yield strength — ensuring our customers receive nothing short of the best.

Our Products

Premium colour coated steel roofing and cladding solutions for every application

Home ›Products
Product 01

Colour Coated Roofing Sheets

Our flagship product — high-quality pre-painted galvanized steel roofing sheets engineered for superior performance in India's diverse climatic conditions. Manufactured using advanced roll forming technology, these sheets offer excellent structural rigidity and aesthetic appeal.

  • Thickness: 0.30 mm to 0.80 mm (TCT)
  • Steel Grade: G550 / G450 High Tensile
  • Coating: Zinc (60–180 GSM) with PVDF/Polyester paint
  • Profiles: Trapezoidal, Corrugated, Rib Pattern
  • Custom lengths up to 18 meters available
  • Colour range: 20+ standard colours + custom options
Request Quote
Product 02

PPGI / PPGL Sheets

Pre-Painted Galvanized Iron (PPGI) and Pre-Painted Galvalume (PPGL) coils and sheets for roofing, cladding, and secondary structural applications. PPGL sheets offer superior corrosion resistance in coastal and high-humidity environments.

  • PPGI: Zinc coated base with colour coating (GI base)
  • PPGL: Galvalume (55% Al-Zn alloy) base for superior corrosion protection
  • Width: 750 mm to 1250 mm
  • Coating weight: AZ 50, AZ 70, AZ 100, AZ 150
  • Applications: Roofing, walling, rain water goods, structural
  • Ideal for coastal and high-humidity regions
Request Quote
Product 03

Industrial Roofing Panels

Heavy-duty roofing panels designed for large-span industrial structures, factories, and warehouses. These panels offer superior load-bearing capacity and are engineered for wind and seismic load resistance.

  • Panel height: 25 mm to 75 mm rib height
  • High tensile steel: G550 grade for maximum strength
  • Wind uplift tested to 200 km/h equivalent
  • Compatible with purlin spacing up to 2.5 m
  • Excellent thermal performance options available
  • Suitable for cold storage, food processing, pharma
Request Quote
Product 04

Wall Cladding Sheets

Precision-engineered wall cladding panels that transform the exterior aesthetics of industrial and commercial buildings while providing outstanding weathering and impact resistance. Ideal for corporate offices, retail fronts, and factory facades.

  • Profiles: Flat, Linear, Shadow-line, Corrugated
  • Thickness: 0.40 mm to 0.70 mm
  • Low-gloss and high-gloss finish options
  • UV resistant PVDF coating for 15+ year colour retention
  • Lightweight — reduces structural load
  • Easy installation with proprietary fixing system

Our Applications

DVR Fab Tech roofing solutions powering India's most important structures

Industries We Serve

Built for Every Sector

From rural grain processing to modern urban retail — our roofing solutions perform under every condition.

🌾 Rice Mills & Agro Processing

Rice mills demand roofing that can withstand high humidity, dust, and constant internal heat. Our colour coated sheets provide excellent moisture resistance and are designed for wide-span mill buildings — maintaining a dry, ventilated interior that protects grain quality.

📦 Warehouses & Logistics Hubs

Modern warehouses require large, unobstructed clear spans. DVR Fab Tech panels offer the structural integrity needed for wide-bay construction, with superior insulation options to maintain stable internal temperatures critical for goods storage.

🏪 D-Mart & Retail Buildings

Retail environments need roofing that looks good and performs well. Our pre-painted sheets in custom brand colours provide the aesthetic appeal retail developers demand, while our structural panels handle the large roof spans common in hypermarket formats.

🏭 Industrial Sheds

From small workshop sheds to large manufacturing bays, our roofing panels provide a cost-effective, durable solution. Our roll-formed profiles ensure strength-to-weight efficiency, reducing structural steel requirements significantly.

⚙️ Factories & Manufacturing Plants

Factory environments with machinery, chemicals, and industrial processes demand roofing that can stand up to years of heavy use. DVR Fab Tech's high-tensile steel panels with chemical-resistant coatings are the industrial-grade choice.

🏢 Commercial Complexes

Modern commercial properties use steel roofing and cladding as a design and branding element. Our range of architectural profiles and finishes allow developers to create striking facades and rooflines that stand out in competitive markets.

❄️ Cold Storage & Food Processing

Cold storage facilities require roofing with exceptional thermal and moisture performance. Our PPGL sheets with high-grade coatings resist condensation, corrosion, and temperature cycling — making them the ideal choice for pharma, food, and dairy cold chains.

🐓 Poultry, Farms & Agri Structures

Agricultural buildings need roofing that provides ventilation, resists moisture, and stands up to years of outdoor exposure. DVR Fab Tech colour coated sheets are widely used for poultry houses, equipment sheds, grain storage, and farm infrastructure across India.

🚗 Automobile Showrooms & Service Centres

Automobile dealerships and service centres demand high-quality wall cladding and roofing that reflects brand standards. Our architectural colour-coated panels deliver the premium look auto brands require while offering durability and ease of maintenance.
